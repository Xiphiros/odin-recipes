Dango Milk (Return)

Ingredients:

  • Dango (sweet rice dumplings):
    • Glutinous rice flour
    • Water
    • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk:
    • Fresh Milk
    • Sweetener (sugar or honey)
    • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Toppings (optional):
    • Kinako (roasted soybean flour)
    • Red bean paste
    • Fruit slices

Instructions

  1. Prepare Dango: Mix glutinous rice flour with water, form into balls, and boil until they float. Cool them in ice water.
  2. Prepare Milk: Heat milk with sweetener and vanilla extract (if using).
  3. Combine: Place dango in a cup and pour warm milk over them.
  4. Serve: Add optional toppings and enjoy warm or chilled.

For more details, visit the Dango Milk page.

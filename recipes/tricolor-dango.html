Tricolor Dango (Return)

Ingredients:

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Food coloring (pink and green)
  • Bamboo skewers

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix glutinous rice flour with water to form a smooth dough. Divide into three portions.
  2. Color Dough: Leave one portion white, color one pink, and the other green using food coloring.
  3. Form Dango: Roll each colored dough into small balls.
  4. Cook Dango: Boil the dango until they float, then cool them in ice water.
  5. Skewer & Serve: Skewer one of each color onto bamboo sticks and serve.

For more details, visit the Tricolor Dango page.

Unagi Chazuke (Return)

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Unagi (eel)
  • Cooked white rice
  • Green tea
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Nori (seaweed) strips
  • Pickled plums or vegetables (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like shiso or green onions)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Unagi: Grill the fresh eel until it's fully cooked and slightly caramelized. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Assemble: Place a serving of rice in a bowl. Top with unagi pieces.
  3. Pour Tea: Brew green tea and pour over the rice and unagi.
  4. Garnish: Add soy sauce, wasabi, nori strips, and optional pickled plums or vegetables.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy immediately.
